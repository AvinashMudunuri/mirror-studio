## Synopsis

Mr. Alvarez announces a school-wide Passion Project Showcase, and the pressure it puts on Alex's most guarded creation — a hand-drawn invented world — forces Wren to choose, again and again, between the ease of Maya's orbit and the harder work of actually showing up for someone. When a private page gets leaked for a laugh, Wren learns that loyalty isn't a single grand gesture; it's a string of small, costly choices nobody else may ever notice her make.

**Themes:** Inclusion, Social Status, Loyalty
