## Synopsis

On her first day at a new school, the player must navigate unfamiliar social terrain — deciding how to introduce herself, where to sit at lunch, and how to treat a fellow outsider named Jordan. Small choices about confidence, kindness, and fitting in ripple forward into a semester-long project partnership — and the partnership itself now visibly carries the weight of what happened at lunch, whether that's a head start on friendship, a blank slate, or something the player has to own up to.

**Themes:** Belonging, Authenticity, First Impressions
